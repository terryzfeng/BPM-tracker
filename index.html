Real-Time BPM Detector




This sketch was made under 72hrs during the first "The Sound of AI Hackathon", Terry Feng developed it with the support of Hegel Pedroza, Sabine Gund, and Apostolos Ntelopoulos. It can detect the immediate and running BPM of a sound file and generate a visualization of the tempo. It uses mainly P5js for all the tasks.
